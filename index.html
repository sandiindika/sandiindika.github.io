Automatic Text Summarization on Health Articles
Using Maximum Marginal Relevance




Automated text summarization is nowadays a hot topic that is widely discussed in Machine Learning as well as Natural Language Processing. One of the advantages of automatic text summarization is that it saves a lot of people's time in reading a book, from journals to news articles.

I created this research to try to apply the Maximum Marginal Relevance (MMR) method to the application of automatic text summarization, especially in articles about health in Indonesian.
